Capabilities

What PainOnSocial Does

Four core engines working together to turn noisy social discussions into structured, actionable product intelligence.

Pain Discovery

Continuously scans Reddit for complaints, frustrations, and unmet needs. NLP extracts the core pain behind every rant.

Severity Scoring

Proprietary severity model weighs frequency, intensity, urgency, and willingness-to-pay into a single 0-100 score.

Cluster Mapping

Groups related pain points into thematic clusters. See which problem areas have the highest concentration of validated pain.

Solution Generator

AI generates targeted solution concepts with market sizing, competitor landscape, and go-to-market hypotheses.
