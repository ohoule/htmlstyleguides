Features

Every tool you need to go from raw community signals to validated product ideas.

Pain Discovery

Automated scanning of Reddit threads to extract genuine user complaints, frustrations, and unmet needs using fine-tuned NLP models.

Severity Scoring

Each pain point receives a composite severity score based on frequency, emotional intensity, and willingness-to-pay signals in the data.

Solution Generation

AI-generated solution blueprints complete with feature specs, competitive analysis, and go-to-market strategy drafts.

Community Intel

Track emerging pain points across subreddits in real-time. Set alerts for new complaint patterns and competitive mentions.
