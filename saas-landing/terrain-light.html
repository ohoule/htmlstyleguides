04 — Features
Everything you need to find real pain

Four core capabilities that transform Reddit noise into validated business opportunities.

Pain Discovery Engine

Automatically scans Reddit discussions to identify genuine pain points people express in their own words. No surveys, no bias.

Severity Scoring

AI assigns severity scores based on frequency, emotional intensity, and willingness to pay. Prioritize what matters most.

Solution Generator

Transforms validated pain points into concrete solution ideas with market sizing, competitive analysis, and go-to-market hints.

Community Mapping

Track which subreddits cluster around specific pain points. Understand community dynamics and where your audience gathers.
