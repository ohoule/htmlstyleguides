04 Features
How Cortex works
Four modules. One pipeline. From community noise to validated opportunity in minutes.
01

Community Discovery

Browse 800+ curated Reddit communities across industries. AI pre-filters for business-relevant discussions with high pain signal density.

02

Pain Point Analysis

AI severity scoring from 1-10, frequency tracking, and sentiment analysis. Ranked by validation strength and market opportunity size.

03

Solution Ideation

10 AI-generated solution concepts per pain point. Each includes market sizing estimate, complexity rating, and competitive landscape snapshot.

04

Evidence Trail

Every insight linked to source Reddit quotes with permalinks. Full audit trail for your research. Export-ready for stakeholder presentations.
