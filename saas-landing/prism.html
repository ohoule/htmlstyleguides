04 — Features

How PainOnSocial Works

Four precise steps from raw community noise to validated, scored business opportunities.

01
Community Discovery

AI scans Reddit, filtering thousands of subreddits to find communities where your target audience congregates and expresses frustrations.

02
Pain Point Analysis

NLP models extract pain points from discussions, categorize them by theme, and assign severity scores based on frequency and emotional intensity.

03
Solution Ideation

Generative AI proposes product/feature concepts that directly address the highest-severity pain points, with market size estimates.

04
Evidence Trail

Every insight links back to source discussions. Verify claims, read context, and build conviction before committing resources.
