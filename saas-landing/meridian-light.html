§04 Features

Map the terrain of real problems

Four powerful tools to navigate from raw conversations to validated opportunities.

Pain Discovery

AI scans thousands of Reddit threads to find recurring pain points people actually describe — not hypothetical problems, but real frustrations with severity scores.

Severity Scoring

Each pain point receives a severity score based on frequency, emotional intensity, and market willingness to pay — so you focus on what truly matters.

Solution Generation

For every validated pain, PainOnSocial generates solution blueprints, competitive landscapes, and go-to-market angles you can act on immediately.

Trend Mapping

Track how pain points evolve over time with topographic trend maps. See which problems are growing, which are fading, and where the next opportunity sits.
