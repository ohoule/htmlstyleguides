09 Design Principles

Core values that guide the Ledger design system.

01
Precision First
Every number must be accurate and properly formatted. Financial data leaves no room for ambiguity or error.
02
Trust Through Clarity
Clear visual hierarchy and consistent patterns build user confidence in the accuracy of financial information.
03
Balanced Alignment
Numbers align right, text aligns left. Columns are scannable and comparable. Debits and credits are clearly distinguished.
04
Complete Audit Trail
Every action should be traceable. Design supports transparency, accountability, and regulatory compliance.
