09 Design Principles

Guiding values for the Chambers design system.

I
Authority
Design should convey expertise and trustworthiness befitting the legal profession.
II
Precision
Every detail matters. Layouts should be exact, typography meticulous, spacing consistent.
III
Clarity
Complex legal information must be presented with crystal-clear hierarchy and organization.
IV
Tradition
Honor the gravitas of law while embracing modern usability standards.
