Lavender Honey Scones

A cottagecore classic for afternoon tea

15 min
Prep
20 min
Bake
8
Scones

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp dried lavender
  • 1/2 cup cold butter
  • 3/4 cup cream

Instructions

  1. Mix flour and lavender in a large bowl
  2. Cut in cold butter until crumbly
  3. Add honey and cream, mix gently
  4. Bake at 400Â°F until golden
