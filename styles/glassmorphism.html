Design Principles

The foundational concepts guiding the Aurora design system.

01

Depth Through Transparency

Use blur and opacity to create layers that reveal content beneath, adding dimensional richness.

02

Subtle Illumination

Let colors glow softly rather than dominate. Light should feel ambient and atmospheric.

03

Graceful Motion

Animations should feel natural and fluid, enhancing the sense of physical glass surfaces.

04

Accessible Contrast

Despite transparency, maintain readable text and clear interactive states for all users.

05

Cohesive Atmosphere

Every element should feel like it belongs in the same luminous, ethereal space.

06

Purposeful Restraint

Use glass effects deliberately. Not everything needs to be transparent to be beautiful.
