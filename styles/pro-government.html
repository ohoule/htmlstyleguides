09 Design Principles

01
Accessible by Default
Every component meets WCAG 2.1 AA standards. Accessibility is not optional.
02
Plain Language
Use clear, simple language that all citizens can understand regardless of education level.
03
Mobile-First
Many citizens access services on mobile devices. Design for small screens first.
04
Trust & Transparency
Be clear about data collection, processing times, and what users can expect.
