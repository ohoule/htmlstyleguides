09

Design Principles

Core values guiding every design decision.

01
Accessible by Default
Every component meets WCAG 2.1 AA standards. Accessibility is not an afterthought but a fundamental requirement.
02
Plain Language
Use clear, simple language that all citizens can understand, regardless of education level or native language.
03
Trust & Transparency
Be clear about data collection, processing times, and what users can expect at every step of their journey.
04
Mobile-First
Many citizens access services on mobile devices. Design for small screens first, then enhance for larger displays.
