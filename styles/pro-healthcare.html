09 Design Principles

Guiding values for healthcare interface design.

01
Patient Safety First
Every design decision prioritizes patient safety. Critical information is always visible and errors are prevented through clear validation.
02
Calm & Reassuring
Healthcare environments can be stressful. Our design choices aim to reduce anxiety through soft colors and clear information hierarchy.
03
Clinical Efficiency
Healthcare professionals need to work quickly. Optimize workflows, reduce clicks, and present the right information at the right time.
04
Accessible to All
Patients and staff have diverse abilities. WCAG compliance is mandatory, with special attention to color contrast and screen reader support.
