Design Principles

Rules. Not suggestions.

Raw Honesty

Show the structure. Hide nothing. The interface is what it is—no false facades or decorative lies.

Function First

Every element must earn its place. If it doesn't serve a function, it doesn't exist.

No Ornament

Decoration is crime. Rounded corners are compromise. Sharp edges are truth.

Contrast Is King

Black and white. On and off. Yes and no. Gray areas are for the undecided.

Exposed Grid

The structure is the aesthetic. Borders are features. Spacing is intentional.

Instant Response

No easing. No transitions. Action triggers reaction. Immediately.

Animations

Movement without smoothness. Harsh. Immediate. Intentional.
