09. Design Principles

The rules of radical design.

01

Reject Minimalism

More is more. Layer shapes, colors, and patterns until it feels alive.

02

Embrace Contrast

Put colors that clash next to each other. Make it vibrate.

03

Geometric Chaos

Circles, triangles, squiggles—throw them all in and let them party.

04

Playful Always

Design should spark joy. If it doesn't make you smile, add more shapes.

05

Bold Typography

Headlines that shout. Text that demands attention. Never whisper.

06

Break the Grid

Rules are suggestions. Overlap, rotate, and let elements breathe freely.
